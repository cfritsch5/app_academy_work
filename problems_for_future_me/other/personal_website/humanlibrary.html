The Human Library

A Book is More Than It's Title | A life Is More Than It's Label

The Human Library is a project that aims to start conversations in the community to better understand the other side of stereotypes, discrimination, and bias. Each event gathers readers, general members of the community, and books, people in the communtiy who have a life experience or identity that is misunderstood in our society, to have respectful and open conversations. I volunteered independantly in March, and created a partnership between The Human Library Chicago and The Residence and Greek Life office at Illinois Institute of Technology to set up the collaborative event on campus in April. I participated as a book in both the March and April 2015 events talking about my expeirience being transgender.
